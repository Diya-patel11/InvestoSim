Opening Your First Brokerage Account

Your brokerage account is your gateway to the stock market. Follow this step-by-step guide to open your first account with confidence.

Preparation Checklist

Documents You'll Need

  • PAN card (mandatory)
  • Aadhaar card (for eKYC)
  • Bank account details
  • Canceled cheque or bank statement
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Income proof (for derivatives)

Choosing a Broker

Online Account Opening Process

Step 1: Registration

Visit broker's website and click "Open Account"

Step 2: eKYC

Complete Aadhaar verification with OTP

Step 3: Bank Linking

Add your bank account for fund transfers

Step 4: In-Person Verification

Some brokers require video call or physical verification

Pro Tip: Most accounts open within 24-72 hours. Start with a small amount to test the platform before making larger investments.
