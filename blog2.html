How to Start Investing?

Investing might seem complicated, but getting started is easier than you think. Follow these steps to begin your investment journey.

Steps to Open a Demat & Trading Account

1

Choose a Broker

Select a SEBI-registered stockbroker like Zerodha, Upstox, or Groww.

2

Complete KYC

Provide PAN card, Aadhaar, bank details, and other necessary documents.

3

Sign Up Online

Most brokers allow you to complete the registration online within minutes.

4

Link Your Bank Account

This enables seamless fund transfers for trading.

5

Start Trading

Once verified, you can buy and sell stocks.

Choosing Your First Investment

  • Start with Blue-Chip Stocks: Companies like Tata, Reliance, or Infosys offer stability.
  • Consider Index Funds: If you're unsure, investing in an index fund like Nifty 50 provides diversification.
  • Look at Company Fundamentals: Check revenue, profits, and market trends before investing.
  • Invest Small Initially: Start with a small amount and gradually increase as you gain confidence.

Risk vs. Reward

Low Risk
