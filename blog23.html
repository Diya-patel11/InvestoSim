Tax Implications of Investing

Taxes can significantly impact your investment returns. Understand how different investment vehicles are taxed in India to optimize your after-tax gains.

Capital Gains Tax

Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG)

For equity holdings sold within 12 months:

  • 15% tax on profits (plus cess and surcharge)
  • Applies regardless of amount
  • No indexation benefit

Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG)

For equity holdings sold after 12 months:

  • 10% tax on gains above ₹1 lakh/year
  • First ₹1 lakh is tax-free
  • No indexation benefit for equity

Dividend Taxation

New Dividend Tax Rules

Dividends are now taxed at the investor's income tax slab rate:

  • Company pays DDT (Dividend Distribution Tax) before 2020
  • Now treated as income in hands of receiver
  • TDS @10% if dividend exceeds ₹5,000/year
